My Cause: Oreo Thins

Oreo's in their original form are delicious and are hard to stop eating. Oreo thins improve on the the original by providing a better ratio of filling and cookie, and delivering it in lovely bite-sized chunks.

I felt this cause was necessary because I once had a teacher that pretended not to like Oreo Thins, but as the year progressed and the class brought him more and more, he changed his mind. The disrespect he showed towards Oreo Thins should never have to happen again.
